Enumeration TransmissionDescriptorState

This enumeration contains the state of consistency between a resource and its associated Transmission Descriptor.

Enumeration Literals
 APPLIED

A TMD is assigned consistently to the resource.
 MISMATCH

A TMD is assigned inconsistently and information about all the mismatched TMD parameters is available.
 NOT_APPLICABLE

No TMD is assigned to the resource.
 PENDING

A TMD is assigned and the corresponding resource configuration is in progress.
 TMD_MISSING

A TMD is assigned but the TMD itself cannot be retrieved by the target OS.
