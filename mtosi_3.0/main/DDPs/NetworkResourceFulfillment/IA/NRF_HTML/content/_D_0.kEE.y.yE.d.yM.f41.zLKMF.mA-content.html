Enumeration SubnetworkConnectionState

This enumeration is used to represent the various states that an SNC may take.
The states may be supported by the target OS, depending on the SNC management mode of operation used by the target OS.
See SD1-23 SNC Management Modes Of Operation for details.

Traceability: R_TMF518_NRF_I_0028.

Enumeration Literals
 ACTIVE

The SNC is not in pending state, a route has been assigned to the SNC and all cross-connects for the SNC are active in the network.
 PARTIAL

The SNC is not in pending state, and either a route has not been assigned to the SNC, or not all of the cross-connects of the SNC are active in the network. This may or may not include activated SNCs for which there are currently no active cross-connects in the network, depending on the SNC management mode of operation.
 PENDING

The SNC has been created by a requesting OS and has not been activated by any requesting OS; or the SNC has been successfully deactivated by a requesting OS. That state has no relationship with the network state of the cross- connects of the SNC. It is allowable for a target OS to not support the SNCS_PENDING state and to reject any operation that attempts to put an SNC into SNCS_PENDING state.
