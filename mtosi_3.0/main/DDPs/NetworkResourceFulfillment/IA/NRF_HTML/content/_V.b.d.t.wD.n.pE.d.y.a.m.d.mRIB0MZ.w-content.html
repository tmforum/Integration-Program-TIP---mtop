Enumeration ConnectionDirection

This enumeration represents the direction of an arc, e.g., subnetwork connection, cross-connect, or topological link.

Enumeration Literals
 BI

BIdirectional, i.e. bidirectional TP to bidirectional TP, a.k.a. two-way.
 UNI

UNIdirectional, i.e. source TP to sink TP.
Note: creation of unidirectional connections is supported even when TPs are modelled as bidirectional.
