 getAllMlsnpps

This operation returns all MultiLayer SNPP(s) that are known to the target OS.
The operation returns the MLSNPPs that are pools of SNPs that correspond to CTPs of G.805 TCP type while the SNPPs that group SNPs that represent G.805 CPs are retrieved as a component of the MultiLayer SNPP Link.

The reply may be an empty or a partial list depending upon the visibility that target OS has of the control plane:
- Local & Complete - the target OS completely manages the MultiLayerRoutingArea and the supporting transport plane resources (ME, TP, etc.) such that it can provide a complete view of the MultiLayerSNPPs and all associated transport plane data.
- Local CP Only - the target OS manages the MultiLayerRoutingArea but has no view of the supporting transport plane resources such that it can provide a complete view of the MultiLayerSNPPs but can not provide any associated transport plane data.
- Remote CP Transparent - the target OS manages the MultiLayerRoutingArea at this level but may not have a view of the details of the subordinate MultiLayerRoutingArea levels and has no view of the supporting transport plane resources, such that it can provide a complete view of the MultiLayerSNPPs but not necessarily all of the SNPP aliases and can not provide any associated transport plane data.
Remote CP Limited - the target OS partially manages the MultiLayerRoutingArea at this level and may not have a view of the details of the subordinate MultiLayerRoutingArea levels and has no view of the supporting transport plane resources, such that it can provide a view of some of the MultiLayerSNPPs and not necessarily all of the subordinate SNPP for those MultiLayerSNPPs and can not provide any associated transport plane data.
- Remote CP Opaque: The target OS is unable to provide any information related to MultiLayerSNPPs for the specified MultiLayerRoutingArea

Traceability: R_TMF518_MRI_II_0198, UC_TMF518_MRI_0029.
