 getAllEquipment

This operation returns all Equipment and Equipment Holders that are contained in a provided Managed Element or Equipment Holder.

Traceability: R_TMF518_MRI_II_0085, R_TMF518_MRI_II_0088.
 getAllSupportingEquipment

This operation returns all Equipment that supports a provided Physical Termination Point (PTP) or Floating Termination Point (FTP).
For a PTP, the Equipment that are returned are those which support the physical layer of the PTP (i.e. implements the port or are connected by a fibre, wire, etc.).
For an FTP, the Equipment returned are those which support the physical circuitry implementing the FTP and will include the Equipment from which the FTP is named (the FTPs will depend upon the presence of that Equipment for their most basic operation).
For a particular PTP/FTP the Tx port/function and Rx port/function may be on different cards and in this case both should be returned. Equipment that are used by the PTPs/FTPs, but that do not support them directly (such as a shared DEMUX card) are not reported.
In case of Equipment Protection, the operation reports the primary Equipment only; i.e., the protecting Equipment will not be returned.

Traceability: R_TMF518_MRI_II_0083.
 getContainedEquipment

This operation returns the Equipment and Equipment Holders directly contained by a provided Equipment Holder.
The operation differs from getAllEquipment() in that it only looks at the next level of the containment hierachy.

Traceability: R_TMF518_MRI_II_0087.
